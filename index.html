About ReWearX

ReWearX was created by a passionate team of students who wanted to make sustainable fashion accessible to everyone. Our mission is to reduce textile waste by creating a circular economy for clothing.

We believe that every small action contributes to a larger impact. By swapping instead of shopping new, we can collectively make a difference in reducing the fashion industry's environmental footprint.
