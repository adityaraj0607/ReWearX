How ReWearX Works

Simple steps to start your sustainable fashion journey

1. Upload Items

Share your pre-loved clothing items with our community. Upload photos, set conditions, and earn EcoPoints.

2. Swap or Redeem

Browse amazing items and either swap with your clothes or redeem using your earned EcoPoints.

3. Love & Share

Enjoy your new items and continue the cycle of sustainable fashion. Rate and review your experience.
